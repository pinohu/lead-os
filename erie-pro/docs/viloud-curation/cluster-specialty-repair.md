## Erie-specific filter

- **Chimney content** — heavy emphasis on wood-burning fireplaces (more common in Erie than most regions). Creosote buildup is a real winter concern
- **Garage door winter issues** — cold-weather seal failures, opener cold-start issues. Common Erie callouts
- **Appliance** — energy-efficiency content matters more in Erie (high heating bills)
- **Locksmith** — emergency lockout content less seasonal; winter overnight lockouts have higher urgency

## Channel branding

- **Name:** `Erie Specialty Repair TV`
- **Description:** `Appliance, garage door, chimney, locksmith, and handyman content for Erie homeowners.`
- **Schedule:** Linear loop

## Sub-niche specializations

- **`appliance-repair`** — biggest content pool. Major brands (LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, GE) all have repair channels worth pulling from
- **`garage-door`** — high-emergency content; springs are particularly dangerous. Safety messaging should be prominent
- **`chimney` / `fireplace`** — split if data warrants (chimney = inspection/cleaning; fireplace = insert install, gas conversion)
- **`locksmith`** — split lock-security from lock-emergency content. Emergency content (lockouts, break-in repair) has urgent intent
- **`handyman`** — generalist; cross-pollinates with most other clusters. Use videos that show "small projects bundled" — the value proposition of a handyman
