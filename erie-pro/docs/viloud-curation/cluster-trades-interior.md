## Erie-specific filter

Interior trades in Erie are mostly climate-agnostic, but a few considerations:

- **Basement finishing** — Erie's high basement-flooding risk means waterproofing/dehumidification context matters more than in dry climates
- **Pre-1950 housing** — plaster (not drywall), original hardwood, and old wiring complicate remodels. Prefer content that addresses older homes
- **Humidity** — Erie summers are humid; flooring (especially hardwood) and paint timing need humidity awareness
- **Winter installation** — many materials require minimum temperatures; avoid content that assumes year-round flexibility

## Sub-niche specializations

- **`drywall` / `plaster`** — separate skills; plaster (older homes) is becoming rare and commands premium rates. Vet videos accordingly.
- **`epoxy-flooring`** — distinct sub-specialty (garage floors, basement floors, commercial). Lower video volume online; supplement with manufacturer content.
- **`cabinet-refinishing`** — three options on the table (refinish, reface, replace) — content should clearly distinguish.
- **`closet-storage-systems`** — niche; supplement with California Closets and similar manufacturer YouTube channels for design content.
- **`kitchen-remodeling`**, **`bathroom-remodeling`**, **`basement-finishing`** — these are the biggest-budget niches in the cluster. Worth split-out into their own channel in Phase 2 if data supports.
