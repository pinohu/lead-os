## Erie-specific filter

Erie's freeze-thaw cycles destroy exterior trades faster than warmer climates. Prioritize content that addresses:

- **Concrete sealing** ‚Äî Erie concrete needs resealing more often than national average
- **Frost heave** on fence posts, driveways, sidewalks
- **Air-leak detection** in windows (Erie heating bills are high)
- **Storm windows** ‚Äî relevant in older Erie homes
- **Masonry repointing** ‚Äî Erie freeze-thaw breaks down mortar faster

Skip:

- Stucco-focused content (rare in Erie)
- Stamped-concrete content from desert climates (cracking patterns differ)
- Hurricane-shutter window content (irrelevant for Erie)
