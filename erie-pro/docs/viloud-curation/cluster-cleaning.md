## Erie-specific filter

Mostly climate-agnostic, but:

- **Salt residue cleaning** — Erie winters bring road salt into homes; rugs and entryways get hammered
- **Mud-season cleaning** — Mar-May creates heavy cleaning demand
- **Lakefront properties** — sand, mildew, humidity = different cleaning needs than inland
- **Pressure-washing season** — Erie's freeze means pressure-washing is May-October only

Skip content that:

- Focuses on dust-storm cleaning (Southwest)
- Assumes year-round outdoor washing season

## Sub-niche specializations

- **`cleaning`** — generic residential; biggest content pool
- **`carpet-cleaning`** — specialized; consider separate channel in Phase 2 if data justifies (different audience: homeowners reacting to pet stains, post-illness, etc.)
- **`pressure-washing`** — seasonal but high-intent; very visual content (before/after)
- **`commercial-cleaning`** — different audience (B2B). May warrant dedicated channel if directory gets commercial-cleaning leads
- **`rental-turnover-cleaning`** / **`vacation-rental-cleaning`** — overlap; both serve property managers/Airbnb hosts. Cross-pollinate with airbnb-property-management content (which lives in professional-services cluster)
