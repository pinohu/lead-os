## Erie-specific filter

- **PA State Inspections** are mandatory annually — major content angle
- **Cold weather** — battery failures, oil thickening, frozen door locks, salt corrosion. Heavy Erie relevance
- **Salt damage** — road salt eats undercarriage. Rust prevention is a real Erie concern
- **Lake-effect emergencies** — heavy snow stranding scenarios, when to abandon vehicle

Skip:

- Hot-climate content (overheating, cooling system that doesn't apply to Erie winters)
- No-inspection-state content (some states have no annual inspection — different consumer relationship with mechanics)
- Tropical / island specific issues

## Sub-niche specializations

- **`auto-repair`** vs. **`auto-body`** — different audiences. Repair = mechanical (engine, brakes); body = collision/cosmetic. Could split in Phase 2 if data warrants
- **`mechanic`** — same as auto-repair, just different search intent. Share channel
- **`towing`** — different from auto-repair (emergency vs. scheduled). Some content should be split: tow-focused videos in their own programming block (block 4)

If body shop leads have different audience behavior than mechanic leads, consider Phase 2 split. Towing is small but distinctly emergency-driven — share for Phase 1.
