## Erie-specific filter

This cluster has STRONG Erie relevance:

- **Septic** — many rural Erie homes are on septic; municipal sewer doesn't extend everywhere
- **Foundation** — pre-1950 housing stock = old foundations, stone/block construction, common settlement issues
- **Basement waterproofing** — high water table around Erie, lake-effect rain events, frequent basement-water complaints
- **Radon** — PA has HIGHER-than-average radon levels. Erie County specifically has elevated radon risk
- **Well water** — rural Erie County has many private wells; testing is mandatory at home sale

Prefer content that:

- Addresses cold-climate freeze-thaw on foundations
- Covers septic in cold climates (frozen drain fields are real)
- Discusses PA radon levels and laws specifically

Skip:

- Desert-climate basement content (different moisture model)
- Pier-and-beam foundation content (rare in Erie; mostly slab and basement)
- Cistern-based water content (rare in PA)
