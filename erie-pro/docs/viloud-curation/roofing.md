## Erie-specific filter

Roofing in Erie is uniquely punishing:

- **Lake-effect snow loads** can exceed 60 pounds per square foot — most southern-US roofing content doesn't cover snow-load engineering
- **60-80 freeze-thaw cycles per winter** stress flashing and shingles harder than anywhere south of the snow belt
- **Ice dam risk is real** — Erie homes routinely see ice dam damage
- **Storm chasers** descend after every major event — the Roofing Insights channel has extensive coverage of storm-chaser scams, which is directly relevant

Prefer videos that:

- Cover cold-climate roofing specifically
- Address ice dam prevention and repair
- Warn about storm-chaser tactics
- Discuss snow guards, heat tape, ice & water shield
