## Erie-specific filter

PA-specific content is valuable here:

- **PA bar association** lawyer-finder resources
- **PA tax** specifics — local taxes, school district taxes (uncommon in many states)
- **PA real estate** — disclosure forms specific to PA, RELRA rules
- **Erie real estate market** — lake-effect impact on lakefront properties, lake-erosion zones
- **PA inheritance tax** — unique tax that most national content doesn't address

Skip:

- State-specific content from CA, TX, FL, NY, etc. (different laws)
- Federal-only content when state nuance matters (estate planning, real estate)

## Sub-niche specializations

- **`legal`** — broad; visitors range from "I need a will" to "I'm being sued". Block 2 (when you need them) is crucial
- **`accounting` / `tax`** — seasonal demand spike Jan-April. Consider seasonal programming weighting
- **`real-estate`** — high content volume; could justify dedicated channel
- **`financial-advisors`** — sensitive (people's money). VET CONTENT CAREFULLY for fee-conflict bias
- **`insurance-agents`** — thin YouTube ecosystem; supplement with major-carrier brand content
- **`mortgage-brokers`** — heavily seasonal with rate cycles. Most evergreen: process explainers
- **`property-management`** — landlord-facing audience; different from tenant-facing
- **`airbnb-property-management`** — booming niche; BiggerPockets has strong content here
- **`estate-sale-services`** — thin YouTube ecosystem. Supplement with estate-sale-company walkthroughs
- **`funeral-homes`** — VERY thin (and visitors here are grieving). Consider skipping Viloud entirely; serious tone needed
- **`home-inspection`** — InterNACHI + Home Inspection Insider can fill this

**Recommendation: skip Viloud embed for `funeral-homes`.** Visitors here are in grief and don't need video content. Use the page space for direct contact and gentle messaging instead.
