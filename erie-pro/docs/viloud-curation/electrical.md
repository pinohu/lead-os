## Erie-specific filter

Erie has a high density of pre-1950 housing stock. Many homes still have:

- **Knob-and-tube wiring** (insurance often excludes coverage)
- **60-amp or 100-amp panels** (need upgrade to 200-amp for modern loads)
- **Aluminum branch wiring** from the 1965-1972 era
- **Two-prong outlets** without ground wires

Prefer videos that address these scenarios specifically. They're directly relevant to a meaningful share of Erie homeowners and will resonate more than newer-construction content.
