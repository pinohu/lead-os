## Erie-specific filter

**Heat pumps are tricky in Erie.** Erie winters routinely hit single digits and below; standard heat pumps lose efficiency around 30°F and stop working below 10-15°F without backup heat. Prefer videos that:

- Address cold-climate heat pump performance honestly
- Cover dual-fuel systems (heat pump + gas furnace backup)
- Discuss furnace sizing for cold climates (most southern-US content under-sizes)
- Mention winterization and freeze-up prevention

Skip videos that:

- Recommend heat pumps without backup heat for cold climates
- Focus exclusively on Florida/Texas/Arizona AC concerns (mini-splits, evaporative coolers — irrelevant for Erie)
