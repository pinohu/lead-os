## Phone Script

### Opening

"Hi [NAME], my name is [YOUR NAME]. I run a lead generation service for pest control companies in [CITY]. Do you have 60 seconds?"

*(If yes, continue. If busy, ask for a better time and call back.)*

### Pitch (60 seconds)

"Here is the short version: I capture homeowners who are searching online right now for emergency pest control in [CITY]. They fill out a form with their contact info, pest type, and urgency level. I verify the information, then send the lead exclusively to one provider — not four or five like HomeAdvisor does.

You would pay per lead — $35 to $75 depending on the pest type. No contracts, no monthly minimums. You can pause or cancel anytime.

I would like to send you 2-3 test leads this week at no charge so you can see the quality. Would that work?"

### If They Want More Detail

"Each lead includes:
- Homeowner name, phone, and email — all verified
- Type of pest they are dealing with
- How long they have had the problem
- How urgently they need service
- Their address and whether they own or rent

You get a text and email the moment the lead comes in. Average response time from our providers is under 15 minutes, and we are seeing about a 25% booking rate."

### Closing

"Great. I will set you up in the system today. You will get a text when your first lead comes through — probably within a day or two. If the quality is not what you expected, no hard feelings. Sound good?"

## Objection Handling

### "How do I know the leads are real?"

"Every lead has a verified phone number and email address. The homeowner filled out a form describing their pest problem — these are not scraped contacts or purchased lists. I will send you 2-3 free test leads so you can call them yourself and verify the quality before you pay anything."

### "What if the lead does not convert?"

"You pay for the lead, not the conversion — that is standard in lead gen. But our leads convert at 25%+ because these are people actively searching for emergency pest control right now. They are not cold contacts. Compare that to HomeAdvisor leads that convert at 5-10% because they go to four companies at once. The math works out better even at a higher per-lead price."

### "I already get leads from HomeAdvisor / Angi"

"I hear that a lot. The difference is exclusivity. HomeAdvisor sends the same lead to 4-5 companies, so you are competing on speed and price. My leads go to one company — you. That means no race to call first, no price wars, and a much higher close rate. Most of my providers use me alongside HomeAdvisor and tell me the quality difference is night and day."

### "That is too expensive per lead"

"I understand the concern. Let me break down the math: if you pay $50 per lead and close 25% of them, your cost per acquired customer is $200. If your average job is $300-500, you are making $100-300 per customer — and that is before the annual plan upsell. Most providers tell me the ROI is better than any other channel they use."

### "I do not need more leads right now"

"That is great — means business is going well. Two things to consider: first, you can set a daily cap so you never get more leads than you can handle. Second, slow seasons always come. Getting set up now means you have a reliable lead pipeline ready to turn on when you need it. And the free test leads cost you nothing to try."

### "How do I know you will not send the same lead to my competitor?"

"Leads are 100% exclusive. When a lead comes in for your service area, it goes to you and only you. That is my core differentiator versus the big platforms. I will put that in writing if you want. Exclusive leads are worth more, which is why my pricing is straightforward — you are not competing with three other companies for the same customer."

### "Can I pick which types of leads I get?"

"Absolutely. You can filter by pest type, urgency level, and service area. If you only want termite leads, I will only send you termite leads. If you want everything except bed bugs, we can set that up. You are in full control."

### "What is the cancellation policy?"

"There is no contract. You can pause or stop receiving leads at any time with one text or email. No cancellation fees, no notice period. I keep providers by delivering quality, not by locking them in."

## Provider Qualification Checklist

Before onboarding a provider, verify:

- [ ] Active state pest control license (check state licensing board)
- [ ] General liability insurance ($1M minimum coverage)
- [ ] Online reputation: 3.5+ stars on Google with 20+ reviews
- [ ] Responds to test call within 15 minutes during business hours
- [ ] Confirms service area by zip code
- [ ] Agrees to lead pricing for their desired pest types
- [ ] Provides preferred contact method (call, text, email, or all three)
- [ ] Understands leads are exclusive and time-sensitive
