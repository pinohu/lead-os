## Platform Valuation

### Pre-Revenue (Current State)

| Method | Range | Basis |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Comparable code asset sale** | $75K–$200K | Multi-tenant SaaS: 670 pages, 4,109 tests, 295 API endpoints, 155 lib modules, 22 presets |
| **Development cost replacement** | $250K–$500K | 3-5 person team, 6-12 months at $150-200/hr to rebuild |
| **Strategic acquisition (with 10 clients)** | $500K–$1.5M | 3-5x ARR + code premium |

**Realistic as-is: $100K–$250K** to the right buyer (agency, PE-backed martech rollup, franchise operator).

### With Revenue

Private SaaS multiples in 2026 (source: Flippa, Acquiry):

| ARR | Multiple | Valuation Range |
|-----|----------|----------------|
| $120K ($10K MRR) | 3–5x | $360K–$600K |
| $500K | 4–6x | $2M–$3M |
| $1M | 5–7x | $5M–$7M |
| $3M+ (>30% growth) | 7–10x | $21M–$30M |

Key premium drivers:
- Net Revenue Retention >120% → 30-50% higher multiple
- Rule of 40 score >50 → 7-9x ARR in private transactions
- EBITDA-positive → 20-40% premium over cash-burning comparables

## Pricing by Buyer Persona

### Persona 1: Digital Marketing Agency

**Profile:** Manages 5-50 clients across multiple niches. Currently pays for 8+ SaaS tools. Spends 20+ hrs/wk on manual reporting.

**Value delivered:** Replaces tool sprawl, automates client reporting, enables multi-niche management from one dashboard.

| Tier | Monthly (per client) | Setup Fee | Annual Value to You (15 clients) |
|------|---------------------|-----------|--------------------------------|
| **Conservative** | $500 | $2,500 | $90,000 |
| **Standard** | $1,000 | $5,000 | $180,000 |
| **Premium** | $2,000 | $7,500 | $360,000 |

**Justification:**
- GoHighLevel SaaS resellers charge $297-497/client/month
- Agency retainers in HVAC, legal, dental are $500-2,000/mo (normal)
- Her clients pay her $2K-5K/mo; your platform is her delivery engine
- At $500/mo from you, she keeps $1,500/mo margin per client = $22,500/mo profit on 15 clients

---

### Persona 2: SaaS Entrepreneur

**Profile:** Building a white-label lead gen SaaS. Solo or small team. Wants custom branding, Stripe billing, instant deploy.

**Value delivered:** Fully built platform they rebrand and resell. No code. No development team.

| Tier | Monthly | Setup Fee | Their Potential MRR (50 users) |
|------|---------|-----------|-------------------------------|
| **Conservative** | $99 | $0 | $9,850 (resell at $197/user) |
| **Standard** | $249 | $0 | $9,601 net (resell at $197/user) |
| **Premium** | $499 | $0 | $9,351 net (resell at $197/user) |

**Justification:**
- GoHighLevel starts at $97/mo; Vendasta at $359/mo
- White-label lead gen platforms price $99-499/mo in market
- They resell at $97-497/mo per end-user
- 50 users at $197/mo = $9,850 MRR; your fee is 2.5-5% of their revenue

---

### Persona 3: Lead Generation Company

**Profile:** Buys and sells leads at scale across industries. Needs scoring, marketplace, outcome tracking.

**Value delivered:** AI-scored lead marketplace with temperature pricing. Quality guarantees. Outcome tracking.

| Tier | Monthly | Setup Fee | Their Revenue Potential |
|------|---------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Conservative** | $2,000 | $5,000 | $7,500/mo (100 leads at avg $75) |
| **Standard** | $5,000 | $10,000 | $50,000/mo (500 leads at avg $100) |
| **Premium** | $10,000 | $15,000 | $100,000+/mo (2,000+ leads) |

**Justification:**
- Industry average CPL: $198 (all industries), $320 (legal), $285 (healthcare), $461 (financial services)
- Lead temperature pricing: cold $25, warm $50, hot $100, burning $200
- If they capture at $30 cost and sell at $100-200, margin is 70-85%
- Your $5K/mo fee is <10% of their revenue at standard volume

### Persona 4: Consultant / Implementer

**Profile:** Technology consultant who implements systems for clients. Charges project fees + monthly retainers.

**Value delivered:** Turnkey implementation platform. Client portal, automation, CRM — all pre-built.

| Tier | Monthly | Setup Fee | Their Revenue (5 clients) |
|------|---------|-----------|--------------------------|
| **Conservative** | $1,000 | $3,500 | $15,000/mo ($3K/client retainer) |
| **Standard** | $3,000 | $7,500 | $25,000/mo ($5K/client retainer) |
| **Premium** | $5,000 | $15,000 | $50,000/mo ($10K/client retainer) |

**Justification:**
- Implementation projects: $5K-25K per engagement
- Monthly retainers: $1K-5K/mo per client
- Platform cost at $3K/mo = 12-20% of revenue
- Consultant keeps 80%+ margin

---

### Persona 5: Franchise Network Operator

**Profile:** 50+ franchise locations. Needs centralized lead routing, brand compliance, performance benchmarking.

**Value delivered:** Multi-tenant platform with territory management, compliance automation, and location-level analytics.

| Tier | Monthly (per location) | Setup Fee | MRR (50 locations) |
|------|----------------------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Conservative** | $200 | $10,000 | $10,000 |
| **Standard** | $400 | $20,000 | $20,000 |
| **Premium** | $600 | $30,000 | $30,000 |

**Justification:**
- Franchise tech platforms charge $150-1,000/location/mo
- Each location generating 10 extra leads/mo at $150 avg = $1,500/mo new revenue
- $400/mo fee = 3.7x ROI per location
- Enterprise franchise platforms (FranConnect, etc.) charge $500-1,000/location
